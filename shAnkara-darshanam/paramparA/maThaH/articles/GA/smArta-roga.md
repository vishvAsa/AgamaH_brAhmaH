> AparArka seemed to had some unnecessary hatred toward shaiviam. But not surprising as he was poster boy for smartarogi:

The accursed Aparāditya-1’s own descendants, Mallikārjuna and Aparāditya-2 realized the worthlessness of his inane proclamations and had Saiddhāntika Śaivācāryas as Rājagurus.

The Roga suffered by the likes of Aparāditya-1 was not some isolated instance. Rather, it was part of a larger phenomenon that went on for hundreds of years and did untold damage to the Hindu fabric. Some correction did arrive in the 1600s but it was a case of “too little, too late”.
